Kevinsville is a tiny smart city which is surrounded by multiple waterbodies. The planning of the city is done efficiently and the roads are very accessible and user friendly. Certain rules and regulations have been imposed inorder to prevent misuse of the roads and accidents.

Multiple bridges and flyovers have been constructed inorder to make the routes shorter and areas more accessible.
